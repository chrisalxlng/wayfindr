Features

Features

Navigate to beautiful landscapes

Discover the beauty of nature! Explore stunning landscapes like mountains, waterfalls, forests or deserts. With Wayfindr you can visit thousands of incredible landmarks with ease. Don't know what to see next? No Problem! Just visit the 'Discover' tab in Wayfindr.

Get to your destination with public transport

You are late getting to work or school and need the fastest and easiest way to your destination? Thanks to Wayfindr those situations belong to the past. Just type in the place you need to go and Wayfindr does the rest. You will be there in no time!

Save maps on your phone for offline use

Imagine this: You are on vacation in the middle of nowhere and there is no WiFi nearby. How do you get out of this situation? Just download Wayfindr! It will allow you to download maps of large areas on to your phone so you have acccess to them when you are really in need.
